## Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find answers in these common questions:

### Reading-Related Questions

**Q: How long does a reading take?**
A: Bazi readings typically take 60-90 minutes. Tarot readings range from 30 minutes (single question) to 60 minutes (comprehensive spread).

**Q: Do you offer readings via video call?**
A: Yes! All readings are conducted via Zoom, Google Meet, or your preferred video platform.

**Q: What information do you need for a Bazi reading?**
A: Your exact birth date, time (as accurate as possible), and location. See the [Services](/services/) page for details.

**Q: Can I record our session?**
A: Absolutely! I encourage recording for your reference.

### General Questions

**Q: Do you offer teaching or workshops?**
A: I occasionally offer group workshops on Bazi basics and Tarot. Email me to be notified of upcoming sessions.

**Q: Can you recommend resources for learning Bazi/Tarot?**
A: Yes! Check my blog posts or email me for personalized recommendations based on your level.

**Q: Do you collaborate with other practitioners?**
A: I'm open to collaboration with fellow spiritual practitioners. Email me with your proposal!
